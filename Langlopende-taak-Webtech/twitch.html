What is Twitch?

Twitch is the largest live streaming platform

Twitch is a live streaming platform with a huge reach and a wide range of topics. See it as watching Live TV, but a lot more amatueristic AND there’s live interaction with the streamer by live chat.
Twitch’s content exists mostly out of gaming channels but a lot of streamers also do cooking streams, live tutorials, music and Q&A sessions.
